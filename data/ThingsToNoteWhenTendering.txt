Things to Note When Tendering for Hawker/Market Stall

DO's:
- Submit 1 bid per stall by yourself
- Complete the Form of Tender through FormSG by yourself
- Comply to the 'Declaration on Non-Collusion'

DON'Ts:
- Ask an agent or another person to submit the bid on your behalf
- Discuss or share the tender details (e.g. bid prices, factors used to calculate bid prices, the decision to tender etc.) with family members, friends or anyone else
- Arrange to tender for the stall with another person
